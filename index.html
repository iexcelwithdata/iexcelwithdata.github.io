About Me

I'm a Data & Energy Analytics professional passionate about leveraging data to optimize decision-making in the renewable energy sector. My work focuses on Power BI automation, energy forecasting, and data modeling for market analysis across the UK and EU regions.

I specialize in transforming complex datasets into interactive insights that drive strategy. My portfolio showcases projects in power market forecasting, capture price analytics, and performance tracking for renewable technologies like Solar PV, Onshore Wind, and Offshore Wind.
